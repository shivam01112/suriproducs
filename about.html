What can we do for you ?

What products does SP manufacture?

SP specializes in manufacturing hydraulic brake parts, rubber-to-metal bonded parts, and precision rubber components for a wide range of automotive and industrial applications.

Which markets and industries does SP serve?

SP caters to Tier 1 suppliers and the independent aftermarket across global markets, including Indian, European, Japanese, Chinese, and American vehicle applications. Our components are used in commercial vehicles, trailers, earthmoving, material handling, and agricultural equipment.

Where are SP’s manufacturing facilities located?

SP operates two state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in Delhi NCR and Noida, equipped with modern technology and quality systems to ensure precision, durability, and consistency in every product.

Does SP export its products internationally?

Yes, SP exports globally through its export unit, Suri International, which is recognized by the Government of India as a Star Export House, supplying components to international OEMs and aftermarket clients.
